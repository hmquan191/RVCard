Red Velvet
Red Velvet is a South Korean
K-pop girl group that debuted in 2014. They are known for their dual concept of "red" and "velvet". The group's music reflects this concept, with the "red" side being brighter and more pop, and the "velvet" side being more mature and R&B-influenced.
